TOURS

Visit Taormina

Taormina is a coastal town in Sicily, Italy known for its historical, cultural and natural beauty. It boasts ancient Roman ruins, stunning views of the Ionian Sea and Mount Etna, and a vibrant tourist scene with idyllic beaches and lively nightlife.

Visit Teatro Greco

The Teatro Greco in Taormina, Sicily, is an ancient theater built in the 3rd century BCE by the Greeks and later expanded by the Romans. It is a well-preserved and beautiful theater, with a seating capacity of 15,000 and a unique combination of Greek and Roman architectural styles, offering stunning views of the sea. The theater still hosts performances and events today, showcasing the ancient world's cultural heritage.

Swim at Isola Bella

Isola Bella is a small island located in the Bay of Taormina, Sicily, Italy. The island is famous for its stunning beauty, with a picturesque beach, crystal-clear waters, and lush greenery, as well as its historic significance as a site of a former monastery. Today, Isola Bella is a popular tourist destination, attracting visitors from all over the world with its natural beauty and serene atmosphere.

Hike Etna

Mount Etna is an active volcano located on the east coast of Sicily, Italy. It is one of the most active volcanoes in the world and is one of the largest volcanic structures in Europe. Etna is known for its frequent eruptions and spectacular displays of lava, as well as its breathtaking views from the summit. The mountain is also surrounded by vineyards and fertile farmland, making it an important cultural and economic center for the region.
